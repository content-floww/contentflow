Refund Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

We want you to be completely satisfied with ContentFlow AI. Please read our refund policy carefully to understand your options.

✅ We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all new subscriptions — no questions asked.

1. Money-Back Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with ContentFlow AI within the first 14 days of your initial subscription, you are eligible for a full refund. To request a refund, simply contact us at support@contentflow.ai within this period.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are available under the following conditions:

  • Your refund request is submitted within 14 days of the initial payment.
  • The request is for the first billing cycle only (not for renewal charges).
  • Your account has not been found in violation of our Terms of Service.

3. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued in the following situations:

  • Requests made after the 14-day window has passed.
  • Renewal charges after the first billing cycle.
  • Accounts that have violated our Terms of Service or Acceptable Use Policy.
  • Partial months or unused subscription time after cancellation.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please:

  • Email us at support@contentflow.ai with the subject line: "Refund Request."
  • Include your registered email address and the reason for your request.
  • We will process your refund within 5–10 business days.

5. Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Cancellation stops future charges but does not automatically trigger a refund. Refunds are only issued under the conditions outlined above.

After cancellation, you will retain access to the platform until the end of your current billing period.

6. Payment Processor

All payments and refunds are processed through Paddle, our authorized merchant of record. Refunds will be returned to the original payment method used at the time of purchase.

7. Contact Us

For any billing or refund questions, please reach out to us at: support@contentflow.ai

We're committed to resolving any issues quickly and fairly.
